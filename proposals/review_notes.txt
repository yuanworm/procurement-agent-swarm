FINDING 3 — GST CLAIM IS POTENTIALLY INCORRECT (Medium Risk)
-------------------------------------------------------------
Section 3 states "Prices are inclusive of GST" but the pricing table does not show
a GST line item or the pre-GST base price. Singapore's current GST rate is 9%. If
the $1,200 unit price is ex-GST, the actual total is $6,000 + $540 GST = $6,540
(or $6,002.40 after discount) — materially different from the $5,520 quoted. Quoting
"inclusive of GST" without breaking it out is also non-compliant with standard
GeBIZ quotation formats, which typically require GST to be shown separately.

ACTION: Decide whether $1,200 is inclusive or exclusive of GST. Add a GST line to
the pricing table (e.g., GST 9% = $496.80) and adjust the total accordingly. Align
the footer note to match.
